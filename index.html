Istighfar

A calm tasbih & dhikr counter — on your iPhone, on device.

Istighfar helps you keep your remembrance of Allah throughout the day. Count by tapping, or simply say “Astaghfirullah” and let on‑device voice recognition count for you. A Live Activity, Home Screen and Control Center widgets, custom dhikr, and calm themes keep you focused. No accounts. No cloud. Fully offline.
